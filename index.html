About Me

Steven is a 27-year old student from Waterford, Michigan, who holds a passion for learning.

Steven has developed a mediocre understanding in HTML, CSS, Javascript, as well Java, after taking courses online in his younger years. At a point in his life, he was away from the internet, but regardless, he always told himself that he would eventually become a software developer.

Steven is currently enrolled in a Full-Stack Bootcamp with WeCanCodeIt. Stay tuned on his journey.
